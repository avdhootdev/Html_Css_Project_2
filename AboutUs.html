About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2006, Pro Nature Organic Foods Pvt Ltd has been serving families and building trust for over 17 years. What began as a vision to provide healthier food options, benefit producers, and nurture the planet has grown into a trusted name in organic foods. Based in Bangalore, Pro Nature continues to uphold its commitment to offering pure, wholesome pantry staples crafted with care. Founded by Nidhi Gupta and Varun Gupta, who bring over 15 years of expertise in organic foods, the company is backed by a team of dedicated FMCG professionals and a highly qualified advisory board. Together, they have worked tirelessly to make organic food the first choice for health-conscious and environmentally conscious consumers.
